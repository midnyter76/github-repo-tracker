## What shipped

Added `filter_junk()` in `src/junk.py`, mirroring `src/gaming.py`'s `filter_gamed` pattern exactly. Wired into `src/collector.py` at step 3.5, right after `filter_gamed_fn`, via an injectable `filter_junk_fn=junk.filter_junk` kwarg.

Excludes two recurring junk patterns observed across 2026-07-01 → 2026-07-05 daily reports:
1. Jailbreak/safety-bypass descriptions (deny-list keyword match, English + Chinese)
2. Blank/None descriptions (toggle-gated via `EXCLUDE_BLANK_DESCRIPTION`)
